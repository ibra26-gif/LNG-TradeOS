How explain P&L works
GrossP&L Δ = Σ components:
● Curve move — change in forward price for each index × cargo quantity × (1 − fix_ratio). Only Uncertain exposure contributes.
● Theta / time decay — one day closer to delivery. BOG and discount factors shift slightly. Typically small for LNG.
● Fixing impact — yesterday's index settlement locked in. Uncertain MtM for that day moved to Certain. Delta = (settlement − forward_T1) × qty_fixed_that_day.
● New trades — MTM of any cargo booked today that wasn't in T-1.
● Residual — rounding, freight re-mark, methodology differences.
